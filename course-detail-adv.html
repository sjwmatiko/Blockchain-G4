The Complete Computer Hardware and Components

This course covers the basic components of a computer, including the motherboard, processor, memory, hard disk, and peripherals. Here we will learn how these components work together to create a working computer.

Course Description

Welcome to Computer Hardware and Components Course

The course is designed to provide an overview of the basic components of a computer and their functions. The course will cover topics such as the central processing unit (CPU), memory, storage devices, input and output devices, and computer peripherals. It is suitable for beginners and will consist of ten lectures, each lasting 30 minutes.

In this course, you will learn about the various components that make up a computer and how they work together to perform different functions. You will start by understanding the role of the CPU, which is the brain of the computer, and how it communicates with other components. Then, you will learn about the different types of memory, including RAM and ROM, and how they are used by the computer.

The course will also cover storage devices such as hard drives and solid-state drives, and how data is stored and retrieved from them. You will also learn about input devices such as keyboards, mice, and scanners, and output devices such as monitors and printers.

The course will conclude by discussing various computer peripherals, including speakers, webcams, and microphones, and their functions.

What you’ll learn
  • The role of the CPU in a computer
  • Different types of memory and their functions
  • How data is stored and retrieved from storage devices
  • The functions of input and output devices
  • Various computer peripherals and their roles

At the end of the course, you will have a firm grasp on the fundamental parts of a computer and how they interact. Also, you will be able to recognize and resolve typical hardware problems.
