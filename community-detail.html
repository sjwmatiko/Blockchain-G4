Tanzanian Tech Startups

This community is aimed at bringing together tech startups in Tanzania to share ideas, knowledge, and resources.

The community provides a platform for individuals and startups to showcase their work, network, and collaborate with others in the industry. The community offers various opportunities, such as mentorship, training, funding, and business development programs, to help startups and entrepreneurs grow and succeed.

By joining the Tanzanian Tech Startups community, members can benefit from the collective knowledge, skills, and experiences of others in the community. Members can also get access to various resources and support that can help them develop their skills and grow their businesses

To join

  • One must be a tech startup founder or employee
  • Willing to actively engage in the community by sharing and learning.
  • Use our social media links to join our community.

Members can benefit from

  • Networking opportunities
  • Access to mentors
  • Investors
  • Potential clients
