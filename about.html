About Elimu Digito

We are a team of dedicated individuals who understand the challenges faced by ICT teachers in Tanzania when it comes to delivering content and addressing limited resources.

Our Vision

To provide limitless learning opportunities at the fingertips of every teacher, regardless of their level of digital skills.

Our Mission

To create a digital platform that empowers teachers to learn and prepare teaching content through courses and resources, and organize their teaching notes.

We aim to provide a user-friendly platform with an authentication page for easy sign-up and login, a dashboard for users to access courses and update their profile information, and a teaching notes feature that allows customization according to their preferences.

Additionally, we aim to provide a new feature called General Learnings to help users learn general digital skills without focusing on levels.
